About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Civil Engineering at The University of Texas at Arlington, focusing on Construction Engineering and Management. My research interests include construction engineering, infrastructure systems, construction cost estimation, post-disaster reconstruction, resilience, construction safety, and machine learning applications in civil engineering.

My current research applies data-driven methods, statistical modeling, and machine learning to construction and infrastructure problems. I am particularly interested in unit price estimation, transportation infrastructure, disaster recovery, worker safety training, and AI-supported tools for construction engineering education and practice.
