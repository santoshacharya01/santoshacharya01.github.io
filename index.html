About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Civil Engineering at the University of Texas at Arlington. My research interests include infrastructure systems, machine learning applications in civil engineering, disaster policy, resilience, and data-driven decision-making for civil infrastructure.

My work focuses on using computational methods, statistical modeling, and machine learning to better understand infrastructure performance, community resilience, and policy impacts related to disasters and civil engineering systems.
