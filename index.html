About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Civil Engineering at The University of Texas at Arlington, with a focus on Construction Engineering and Management. My research interests include construction engineering, infrastructure systems, machine learning applications in civil engineering, construction unit price estimation, post-disaster reconstruction, worker safety, and resilience.

My current research focuses on data-driven decision-making in construction and infrastructure systems. I work on topics related to construction cost estimation, transportation infrastructure, disaster recovery, safety training, and artificial intelligence-supported tools for construction engineering education and practice.
