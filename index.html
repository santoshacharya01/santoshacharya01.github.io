About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Civil Engineering at The University of Texas at Arlington, focusing on Construction Engineering and Management. My research interests include construction cost estimation, infrastructure systems, machine learning applications in construction, post-disaster reconstruction, worker safety, and resilience.

My current work focuses on data-driven approaches for construction engineering problems, including unit price estimation, transportation infrastructure, disaster recovery, and technical communication in construction engineering education.
